Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and going to the "My Orders" section. There, you will find real-time updates on your order status.

What is the return policy?

Our return policy offers 14 days for international products and 7 days for domestic products. Please note that some items may not be eligible for return, so we recommend checking the product details before making a purchase.

Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa,Rupay, MasterCard, PayPal, and major online banking options.

How do I cancel an order?

To cancel an order, navigate to the "My Orders" section and select the order you wish to cancel. If the order has already been shipped, you will need to wait for its delivery and then initiate a return. Please note that some products are non-cancellable.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact us via the form below, or email us directly at help@wisitrix.com. We are available 24/7 to assist you.
